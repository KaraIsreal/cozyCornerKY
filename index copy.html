Set Your Brand Ablaze

When it comes to bridging any communication gaps between brands and their audiences, I bring a humorous, insightful and millennial voice to the boardroom table.
Picture this: After countless hours of deliberation, research, and brainstorming, the moment approaches to execute. The stage is set, the audience is presumed ready to receive. Three hours later all preparation falls apart in a tsunami of negative feedback.
The audience is highly dissatisfied, even worse they’re offended. The pain of seeing a campaign self destruct causes one to go back to the drawing board. Enter the rouge (aka yours truly) painting the town through a refined creative lens.
I’m ready to hit the pavement and find the X to the left of the spot, analyzing where the block of communication began to build then churning out a buttercream frosting of replacement ideas that hit the real sweet spot of your audience.
